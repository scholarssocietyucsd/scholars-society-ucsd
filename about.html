About Us

Connection, resources, and leadership for UCSD scholars.

Scholars Society at UC San Diego supports recipients of prestigious merit-based scholarships by building an inclusive community through leadership development, professional growth, and meaningful social engagement.

We host networking mixers, career panels, volunteer opportunities, mentorship programs, and signature social events that help scholars connect with peers and make the most of their time at UC San Diego.
